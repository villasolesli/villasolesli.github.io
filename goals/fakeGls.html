Consumers - How we hurt and how we can help

According to "The Consumer's Guide to Effective Environmental Choices", consumers should prioritize addressing the most harmful activities: car/travel, meat consumption, household construction, and household water use. Another way to get involved as a consumer, is to support animal sancturaries through donations, purchases or volunteer work. Pick a topic to focus on below

Priority Actions for American Consumers

Choose a place to live that reduces the need to drive

Think twice before purchasing another car

Choose a fuel efficient, low-polluting car

Set concrete goals for reducing travel

Eat less meat

Install efficient lighting and appliances

Choose an electricity supplier offering renewable energy

Whenever practical, walk, bike or take public transportation
